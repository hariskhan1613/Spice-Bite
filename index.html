Our Story

Passion on Every Plate

SpiceBite was born from a simple belief — that great food has the power to bring people together. Founded in 2009 by award-winning Chef Marco Reyes, our kitchen blends bold spices from around the world with locally sourced, seasonal ingredients to craft dishes that are as memorable as they are delicious.

Every recipe is a labour of love, refined over years of culinary exploration across Asia, Europe, and the Americas. We invite you to sit back, savour the moment, and let us take you on a journey through flavour.
